Introduction

A new cat requires a slow, patient approach to manage their territorial nature and ensure a peaceful, long-term co-existence. Key steps include separating the new cat initially, scent swapping to build familiarity, supervised visual contact, and using positive reinforcement like treats and play to create good associations.
