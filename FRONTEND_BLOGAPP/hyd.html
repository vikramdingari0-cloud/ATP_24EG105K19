Hyderabad



A Complete List of Things & Places Hyderabad Is Famous For ...Hyderabad is famous for its rich history (Charminar, Golconda Fort), iconic Hyderabadi Biryani, being the "City of Pearls" and diamonds, a thriving IT hub (HITEC City), vibrant culture blending Mughal/Deccani influences, and attractions like Ramoji Film City and Hussain Sagar Lake with its Buddha Statue.
